Should either party fail to fulfill a fundamental obligation under this Agreement, the other party may terminate by providing written notification. Upon Agreement termination, the Assignee must discontinue all usage of the transferred copyright, and all rights and interests in the Work shall revert to the Assignor.

The Assignor affirms and guarantees their status as the exclusive creator and rightful owner of the Work, possessing full authority to transfer the associated copyright.
