II. Subject and Scope of the Agreement

This Agreement governs the determination of all patent and utility model applications pertaining to inventions and materials that may be subject to intellectual property rights, which arise within the scope of the {{projectName}} Project. This project has been developed by the Owner, and this Agreement establishes the rights and obligations of all parties regarding the industrial application and commercialization of the invention.

Any services that may be provided by the Owner to future transferees or licensees following the transfer or licensing of the subject matter, including services related to patents or utility models connected to the invention, are explicitly excluded from this Agreement's scope. Any fees associated with such services are also considered outside the scope of this Agreement.

III. Rights and Obligations of the Parties

The Owner's Obligations:

1. Shall provide the Company with comprehensive documentation, including all technical specifications and information regarding the developed invention, whether patentable or not, in their capacity as the "inventor" as defined by relevant legislation.

2. Acknowledges and agrees that the Company will be the applicant for all domestic and international registration applications.

3. Commits to providing essential technical information for patent application preparation, including claims, figures, and summaries. The Owner will collaborate with designated personnel and assist in document preparation as needed.

4. Shall maintain confidentiality regarding the invention and will not disclose information to third parties without the Company's written consent. The Owner will refrain from any actions that could compromise the parties' rights under this Agreement.

5. Agrees to participate in necessary marketing negotiations when the Company pursues transfers or licenses.

The Company's Obligations:

1. Shall maintain confidentiality regarding the invention and will not disclose information to third parties without the Owner's written consent. The Company will refrain from any actions that could compromise the parties' rights under this Agreement.

2. Will manage all legal procedures to protect the Owner's rights, including signing necessary documentation for patent/utility model applications and commercialization stages, and will keep the Owner informed of relevant developments.

IV. General Provisions

Commercial decisions regarding patent commercialization require written consent from both Parties. If either Party suspects a violation of patent/utility model rights by third parties, they must promptly notify the other Party. The Company will take necessary legal measures at its expense.

In cases of third-party rights infringement during patent use, licensing, or commercialization, liability is shared jointly between the parties.

V. Term of Agreement

This Agreement shall remain effective throughout the duration of the patent protection period unless terminated according to the Agreement's termination provisions.

VI. Termination

The Parties may terminate this Agreement at any time through mutual written consent and agreement on termination consequences.

If either Party fails to meet their obligations and does not remedy the situation within the specified notice period, or if irreparable damage occurs, the affected Party may terminate this Agreement bilaterally.
